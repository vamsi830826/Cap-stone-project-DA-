# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analytics Project

---

## 1️⃣ Project Overview

**Goal:**
Analyze pizza sales data to improve revenue, customer demand planning, and operational efficiency.

**Focus Areas:**

* Orders
* Pizza categories
* Revenue
* Quantity sold
* Time-based trends
* Customer buying patterns

**KPIs designed to support data-driven business decisions for restaurant growth.**

---

## 2️⃣ Business Objectives

* Understand total sales performance
* Identify best-selling and worst-selling pizzas
* Analyze peak order times (daily & monthly trends)
* Monitor category performance (Veg / Non-Veg / Classic / Supreme)
* Improve profitability using data insights

---

## 3️⃣ Data Sources

* Order Details (Order ID, Date, Time)
* Pizza Details (Name, Size, Category, Price)
* Sales Data (Quantity, Total Price)

**Tools Used:**

* SQL (Data extraction & KPI calculation)
* Excel (Validation & Pivot tables)
* Power BI (Interactive Dashboard)
* Tableau (Optional Visualization)

---

## 4️⃣ List of KPIs

### 🍕 Sales KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Pizzas Sold
* Average Order Value
* Average Pizzas per Order

---

### 📊 Category & Product KPIs

* Revenue by Pizza Category
* Revenue by Pizza Size
* Top 5 Best-Selling Pizzas
* Bottom 5 Pizzas
* % Contribution by Category

---

### ⏰ Time-Based KPIs

* Sales by Month
* Sales by Day
* Sales by Hour
* Peak Order Time

---

## 5️⃣ Dashboard

### 🟢 Excel Dashboard

Used for pivot tables and quick KPI summaries.

### 🔵 SQL Work

Used for:

* Data cleaning
* Revenue calculation
* Aggregations
* Group by analysis

### 🟡 Power BI Dashboard

Used for:

* Interactive filters
* KPI Cards

* Category breakdown

---

## 6️⃣ Key Insights

* 🍕 Most revenue comes from **large-sized pizzas**
* 📅 Weekends show higher sales compared to weekdays
* 🕒 Evening hours (6 PM – 9 PM) are peak order times
* 🥇 A few top pizzas generate majority of revenue (Pareto effect)
* 📉 Some pizzas contribute very low revenue and can be optimized

---

## 7️⃣ Recommendations

* Increase promotion of top-selling pizzas
* Bundle low-selling pizzas with combos
* Increase staff during peak hours
* Optimize pricing strategy for medium-size pizzas
* Run seasonal campaigns during low-sales months
